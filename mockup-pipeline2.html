Design notes
0 — Base (fixed)
Stage name shows once. Icon sits top-right, count dominates. Chevrons connect the journey. Clean, ready to ship.
1 — Botanical art
Each card has a faded nature illustration that matches its lifecycle stage. Seed pods → sprout → greenhouse → leaf plant → flower → tree. Subtle but distinctive.
2 — Rich gradient
Saturated gradient per stage with a grain texture layer. More energy and personality without illustrations. Easy to implement, most vibrant feel.
3 — Nature scene
Full SVG scene illustration in each card corner — the most branded option. Visual storytelling per stage. Each card "shows" what that stage means.
